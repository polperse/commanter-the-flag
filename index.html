Fan-made 2v2 Commander siege format

Breach the Wall. Defeat the Keeper. Steal the Flag.

Commander the Flag turns casual Commander into a team objective battle: break the enemy Tower, survive its guardian, carry the Flag home, and hold both banners for three upkeeps.

Invented by Pol Persello · December 19, 2025

The siege loop

How a game is won

The format replaces player life totals with team Towers. You win by controlling both Flags in your Tower long enough for the table to feel the pressure.

1

Break the Wall

Each Tower starts at 20 Wall HP. Attack it, trample through blockers, or aim damage at it.

2

Assault the Keeper

At 0 Wall HP, attackers assault the Flag Keeper. Evasion helps reach it, but never bypasses it.

3

Capture the Flag

After the Keeper falls, any creature with base power 2 or greater can take the enemy Flag.

4

Survive the Retreat

The carrier gains Ward {2}, but the Flag drops if the carrier leaves the battlefield.

5

Hold for 3 Upkeeps

Secure both Flags in your Tower and keep them there for three consecutive team upkeeps.

New in the updated draft

  • Shared team turns now work like a Two-Headed Giant style turn structure.
  • Victory count is checked cleanly after upkeep Flag checks.
  • The Wall cannot be repaired once it has broken for the first time.
  • When a Wall breaks, that team gains 20 Morale.
  • Poison, infect, wither, life loss, and alternate wins are explicitly handled.
